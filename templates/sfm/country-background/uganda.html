{% trans "Police" %}

{% blocktrans %} The Inspector General of Police commands the Uganda Police Force which is divided into Directorates at the headquarters level and operationally into and Police Regions which are further divided into Police Districts, Police Stations, Police Posts and Police Booths. {% endblocktrans %}
