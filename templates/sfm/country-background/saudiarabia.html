{% trans "Security Forces of Saudi Arabia" %}

{% trans "Saudi Arabian National Guard" %}

{% blocktrans %} As of 2009 the King of Saudi Arabia exercised direct control over the Saudi Arabia National Guard (SANG). SANG is divided into two regional commands which command Brigades, as well as independent Brigades which report directly to SANG headquarters. Brigades commanded several Battalions. Additionally there were several independent battalions. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Military" %}

{% blocktrans %} As of 2009 the King of Saudi Arabia was the commander in chief of the armed forces. The chain of command went from the King through the Ministry of Defense and Aviation to the Chief of General Staff who commanded the four branches of the military: the Royal Saudi Land Forces (army), Royal Saudi Air Force, Royal Saudi Navy and Royal Saudi Air Defense Force. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Royal Saudi Air Force" %}

{% blocktrans %} As of 2009 the air force was divided into Wings which command Squadrons. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Royal Saudi Navy" %}

{% blocktrans %} As of 2009 the navy was divided into two fleets - the Western Fleet and Eastern Fleet. {% endblocktrans %}
